Is Kenya a Taskforce Nation?

This resource aims at keeping track of the different task forces aimed at solving different problems ailing kenyans. For decades, the government has set up commissions of enquiries, taskforce, and committees to solve problems that are either caused by incompetence or corruption from public officials.
